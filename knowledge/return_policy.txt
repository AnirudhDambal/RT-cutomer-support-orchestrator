RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. RETURN ELIGIBILITY
   - Products can be returned within 30 days of purchase
   - Items must be unused and in original packaging
   - Return shipping is free for defective items
   - Customer pays return shipping for change of mind returns

2. REFUND PROCESS
   - Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item
   - Original payment method will be credited
   - Shipping costs are non-refundable except for defective products

3. NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
   - Opened software or digital products
   - Personalized or custom-made items
   - Perishable goods
   - Gift cards

4. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
   - Report damaged items within 48 hours of delivery
   - Provide photos of damaged products
   - We will arrange free return shipping and provide full refund or replacement
   - No questions asked for manufacturer defects

5. EXCHANGE POLICY
   - Exchanges available for different sizes or colors
   - Subject to stock availability
   - Exchange requests must be made within 30 days

6. HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
   - Contact customer support via email or phone
   - Provide order number and reason for return
   - Receive return authorization number (RMA)
   - Pack item securely with RMA number
   - Ship to our returns center

For any questions, contact: support@company.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT
