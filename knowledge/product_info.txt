PRODUCT TROUBLESHOOTING
------------------------

Wireless Headphones:
- Won't pair: Reset by holding power button for 10 seconds
- Poor sound quality: Check for firmware updates in companion app
- Short battery life: Disable ANC when not needed

Smart Home Hub:
- Can't connect to WiFi: Ensure 2.4GHz network (5GHz not supported)
- Devices not responding: Restart hub and check device compatibility
- App issues: Update to latest app version

Action Camera:
- Video choppy: Use high-speed SD card (Class 10 or higher)
- Battery draining fast: Disable WiFi when not in use
- Blurry footage: Clean lens, enable image stabilization
