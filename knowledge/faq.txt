FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

ACCOUNT & ORDERS
-----------------

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner, provide your email and create a password. You'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and you'll receive a password reset link within 5 minutes.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Log into your account and go to "My Orders" or use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

Q: Can I modify or cancel my order?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order is processed and cannot be changed. Contact support immediately if needed.

Q: How do I use a promo code?
A: Enter the promo code in the "Discount Code" field at checkout before completing payment.


PAYMENTS
--------

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and do not store your complete credit card information.

Q: Why was my payment declined?
A: Common reasons include insufficient funds, incorrect card details, or bank security holds. Contact your bank or try a different payment method.

Q: When will I be charged?
A: Your card is charged immediately upon order confirmation. For pre-orders, you're charged when the item ships.


PRODUCTS
--------

Q: Are your products authentic?
A: Yes, all products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors.

Q: Do you offer warranty on products?
A: Most products come with manufacturer's warranty. Warranty terms vary by product - check product page for details.

Q: How do I know what size to order?
A: Refer to our size guide on each product page. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up. Contact support for personalized help.

Q: Are products in stock?
A: Product pages show real-time stock availability. If out of stock, you can sign up for restock notifications.


TECHNICAL SUPPORT
-----------------

Q: My product isn't working correctly. What should I do?
A: First, check the product manual or troubleshooting guide. If issues persist, contact our technical support team.

Q: Do you offer installation support?
A: We provide installation guides and video tutorials. Premium installation support is available for select products.

Q: How do I update firmware/software?
A: Instructions are available in the product manual or our support center. Some products update automatically.

----------------

Q: How can I contact customer support?

A: Email responses within 24 hours, phone support immediate, live chat average wait time 2-3 minutes.

A: Yes! 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not satisfied, we'll make it right.


PRIVACY & SECURITY
------------------

Q: How is my personal information used?
A: We use your information only to process orders and improve your experience. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Q: Do you share my information with third parties?
A: We never sell your personal information. We only share with service providers necessary for order fulfillment.

Q: How do I unsubscribe from emails?
A: Click "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any marketing email or manage preferences in your account settings.
